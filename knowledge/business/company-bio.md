# Duck Island Icecream — company bio

**Duck Island Icecream** makes small-batch ice cream with clear flavours and honest ingredients. We focus on seasonal recipes, careful sourcing, and a friendly scoop-shop experience for locals and visitors.

We see ice cream as a simple treat done well: balanced sweetness, real dairy and fruit where it matters, and recipes we are happy to stand behind. Questions about allergens, ingredients, or wholesale can go through our usual shop and business channels. changed
